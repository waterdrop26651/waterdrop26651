### 🔬 **Research Spotlight**

<details open>
<summary><b>🛡️ MM ALIGNER: Safeguarding Multimodal LLMs through Representation Calibration</b> <i>(ACM CCS '26, Under Review)</i></summary>

- **The Problem:** Multimodal LLMs are vulnerable to jailbreaks due to "Representation Drift" (malicious inputs escaping safety boundaries in the embedding space).
- **The Solution:** Proposed a dual-objective optimization framework (Hard lower-bound + Soft upper-bound regularization) to calibrate internal representations.
- **The Impact:** Achieved a **99% rejection rate** against malicious inputs on models like LLaVA and Qwen, with **<2% general capability loss** and zero inference overhead. Surpassed 9 mainstream baselines (e.g., AdaShield, ECSO).
</details>
